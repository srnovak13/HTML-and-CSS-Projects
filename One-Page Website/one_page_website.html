Exploring Ireland

From the bustling metropolis of Dublin to the emerald shores of the Wild Atlantic Way, there is something for everyone in Ireland.
Explore the many castles that dot the countryside, learn to shear sheep in Dingle, fish in the many gorgeous lakes and rivers,
grab a pint in a local pub, learn about the ancient Celtic tribes in the Museum of Ireland...There is always something to do!
