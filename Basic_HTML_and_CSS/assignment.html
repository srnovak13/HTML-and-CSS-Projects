Brief History

Vermont was originally colonized by both New York and New Hampshire in the mid 1700's before declaring its independence in 1777. It was the first state to abolish slavery in its constitution

Fun Facts

Vermont's population is one of the smallest and most rural in the U.S. As late as the 1970's, Vermont had more cows than people. Today, its population barely reaches 600,000.
